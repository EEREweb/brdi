Committee Members

The Biomass Research and Development (BR&D) Technical Advisory Committee (TAC), although established currently under the Food Conservation and Energy Act of 2008, is governed under the rules of the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972 and its Designated Federal Officer (DFO) ensures their advice is objective and accessible to the public. Dr. Ian Rowe, Technology Manager within the U.S. Department of Energy's Bioenergy Technologies Office, is the BR&D TAC's DFO. BR&D TAC Members serve three-year terms and can be renewed only once.
