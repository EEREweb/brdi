ARPA-E FOAs

Plants Engineered to Replace Oil (PETRO): PETRO aims to redirect the processes for energy and carbon dioxide capture in plants toward fuel production. This would create dedicated energy crops that serve as a domestic alternative to petroleum-based fuels and deliver more energy per acre with less processing.
